  • home
  • Ask Sirius

Description:

Ask Sirius is a free platform which allows you to search, preview and download free e-Books (including articles, images, question paper) and videos files into your devices. The platform constantly scan the world wide web to add more free content to their database.

At Ask Sirius they believe that everyone deserves "the right to education and a decent life", so they are passionte about providing free and quality content to the users all over.

Ask Sirius also provide their users the ability to share their original content for other to share in the love.
